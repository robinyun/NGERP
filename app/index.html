    • Expedites performance

    ERP Vision

    To lead our industry as the design-anywhere, buy anywhere, build-anywhere security provider, we need systems that support growth. ERP will streamline information and integrate processes so we can meet or exceed our customers' requirements.

    Why ERP?

    How will ERP benefit Aerospace Systems?

    Over the past several years, Aerospace Systems has been on a journey to shape our sector into a flexible workforce that can Design Anywhere, Build Anywhere, and Support Anywhere. In order to make this a reality, we have developed and are deploying a set of software tools we call the ERP Systems Architecture.

    In July 2012, this enterprise resource planning -- or ERP system -- will replace aging software that we can no longer adequately support. Our current systems will not carry us to the future we seek. For this reason, we are rolling out SAP's MRP (Manufacturing Resource Planning) software for material requirements planning integrated with our MES (Manufacturing Execution System) as our shop floor control system. This migration provides the following advantages to ESMC and our shops as well as the AS sector.

    • Integrates our procurement, scheduling and manufacturing systems for real-time information
    • Provides a platform for us to conduct business seamlessly across the sector irrespective of location
    • Integrates our material requirements planning system into the same software we currently use for corporate financial processes
    • Lowers system maintenance costs

    Basically, by replacing out-dated systems with a common ERP solution of SAP and MES, we'll reduce costs and harmonize practices across our enterprise. ERP gives our company and employees a competitive advantage, positioning us for growth as an industry leader.
